# Complete 11+ Summer Bootcamp — Course Brief

*A daily, live, online 11+ course run over the August holidays — the final run-up to the September grammar exams. This is the plan: what it is, when it runs, what it costs, how it's taught, how it appears on the website, and the roadmap to launch.*

**By:** Phonics to Physics · *Small steps, big results.*

---

## 1. The concept

A **4-week, Monday–Friday, live online bootcamp** that prepares Year 5→6 children for the 11+ across **all four exam components**, finishing days before the September sitting.

- **Not** maths-only or reading-only. The 11+ tests **English, Maths, Verbal Reasoning (VR) and Non-Verbal Reasoning (NVR)** — parents want the whole exam covered, so the course rotates through all four every week and builds difficulty toward a full mock.
- **Cohort-based:** the same group each day, for community, accountability and results.
- **Sold as one programme** (not per day) — better commitment, cashflow and outcomes.
- **Every session recorded** so August holidays/absences don't cause drop-off or refund requests.

### Who it's for
Children entering **Year 6 in September 2026** sitting **state grammar 11+** (early September). *If your local families target independent schools (January 11+), run the same course as a winter bootcamp instead — see §9.*

## 2. Dates & times (2026)

- **Runs:** Monday **3 August** → Friday **28 August 2026** (4 weeks, 20 live sessions).
- **Lands** ~1 week before the early-September grammar exams — the peak-urgency window.
- **Daily time:** **9:30–10:30am** (60 min). Mornings work best — children are fresh and the day is left free. *Optional second cohort at 4:00–5:00pm if demand exceeds one group.*
- **Format:** 45–50 min teaching + 10–15 min live Q&A / worked practice. Short daily homework (self-marking where possible).

---

## 3. Weekly structure (all four components, every week)

Same rhythm each week so parents can see the whole exam is covered; difficulty rises week on week, culminating in a timed mock.

| Day | Focus | Example content |
|---|---|---|
| **Mon** | English | Comprehension technique, vocabulary, spelling/grammar |
| **Tue** | Maths | Arithmetic fluency, word problems, topic of the week |
| **Wed** | Verbal Reasoning | Codes, letter/word logic, sequences |
| **Thu** | Non-Verbal Reasoning | Shapes, rotations, patterns, matrices |
| **Fri** | Mixed timed practice | Exam-style questions under time + group review |

**Week-by-week difficulty arc**
- **Week 1 (3–7 Aug):** Foundations & technique in each component; get the routine going.
- **Week 2 (10–14 Aug):** Core skills; introduce timing on Fridays.
- **Week 3 (17–21 Aug):** Harder questions; exam strategy (guessing, time management).
- **Week 4 (24–28 Aug):** Full timed **mock** midweek + walkthrough; confidence and last-minute technique for the September sit.

## 5. Delivery & tech

- **Zoom Pro** (long meetings, waiting room, breakout rooms). Slides + a screen-share whiteboard.
- **Workbook pack** (PDF) per week; short daily homework, self-marking quizzes where possible.
- **Recordings** posted after each session to the members/course area (or a private link).
- **Signup & payment:** a Stripe "course" product on your site (a bootcamp landing page — §7). Ties into the Phonics to Physics portal; enrolled families get the joining link + workbooks.

---

## 6. Safeguarding (children online — essential)

- **Waiting room on**; admit known registrants only. Unique join link, not public.
- **Parental consent** at signup; a clear **recording & privacy policy** (who sees recordings, how long kept).
- **Cameras optional**; no private 1:1 DMs with children; parents cc'd on all comms.
- DBS status shown on the page for reassurance.

## 7. How it appears on the website

- A **dedicated landing page** — e.g. `phonicstophysics.com/summer-11-plus` — under a **"Holiday Bootcamps"** (or "Courses") nav item.
- A **site-wide announcement bar** during the campaign ("🎒 August 11+ Bootcamp — early-bird ends 17 July → Book") linking to the page.
- Page sections, top to bottom:
  1. **Hero** — name, one-line promise, dates/time, "Book your place" + "See what's included" buttons.
  2. **Urgency strip** — countdown to early-bird deadline / spaces left.
  3. **What's covered** — the four components + the weekly grid.
  4. **Schedule** — dates, daily time, "what a day looks like."
  5. **Why it works / about Chris** — credibility, DBS, results-focused, recordings included.
  6. **Pricing** — standard vs early bird, what's included, sibling discount.
  7. **FAQ** — level, missed sessions, tech, refunds, exam board.
  8. **Final CTA** — book now (early-bird urgency).
- **Booking** = Stripe Checkout for the course product; on payment, the family is enrolled and emailed joining details (reuses the portal's Stripe webhook pattern). A visual mockup is provided: `bootcamp-page.html`.
